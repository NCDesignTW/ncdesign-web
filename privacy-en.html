NC Design CO., LTD. (hereinafter "the Company") values the privacy of every visitor and customer. Scope of This Policy

本隱私權政策適用於您在瀏覽及使用本公司網站服務期間所產生之個人資料處理事宜。請注意，本政策僅適用於本網站之範疇，若您透過本網站連結至其他外部網站，該等網站之隱私權保護事宜，概依各該網站之規範為準，與本公司無涉。

Data Collection & Use

為使您能夠充分享有本網站所提供之互動服務，本公司可能於特定情形下請您提供相關個人資訊，說明如下：

當您使用本網站提供之「詢價表單」、「聯絡我們」或其他互動功能時，本公司將依服務性質請您填寫必要欄位，包含但不限於：公司名稱、聯絡人姓名、職稱、電話號碼及電子郵件地址等。上述資料僅供本公司業務團隊於特定目的範圍內進行處理與使用，未經您明確書面授權，絕不移作其他用途。

除非法令另有規定、取得您的明確同意，或基於回應您詢問之必要，本公司不會將您的個人資料以任何形式轉交予第三方。若有必要委由合作夥伴或服務廠商代為聯繫，本公司將嚴格限制其使用範圍，僅限於提供服務之必要目的。

Third-Party Sharing Principles

本公司承諾不會將您的個人資料出售、出租、交換或以任何形式提供予未經授權之個人或組織，但下列情形除外：

Section 06

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you browse a website. They help the site remember your preferences and interactions, enabling a smoother and more personalized experience on each visit.

How We Use Cookies

This website uses four categories of cookies:

Essential
Required for the website to function correctly. Disabling these cookies will prevent certain core features from working and cannot be turned off via settings.
Analytics
Collect aggregated, anonymized data on visitor behavior — such as frequently visited pages and time spent — used to continuously improve the site experience.
Functional
Remember preferences you have entered (e.g. language selection) to deliver a more personalized experience. These cookies do not track your activity on other websites.
Marketing
Used to record your visit history in order to deliver content or service information relevant to your interests.

Managing Cookie Settings

You may restrict or refuse cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit or prevent some features of this website from functioning properly.
